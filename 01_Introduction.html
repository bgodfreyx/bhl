Introduction

Welcome to the exhibit “For This Is Zion”: Becoming the Lord’s People.

For nearly a century, the people who lived and worked in the Beehive and Lion Houses strove to build Zion. Latter-day Saints around the world continue to build on that legacy.

In this exhibit, you can learn about the ongoing effort to build Zion through four sections featuring original artifacts and inspiring stories. As you move through the exhibit, select the deeper dives that interest you to discover more.

Have fun exploring!
